Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Free Money ("we", "us", "the service", operating at myfreemoney.app) helps you discover class action settlements, unclaimed property, scholarships, government programs, and other money you may be owed. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what we do with it.

Information We Collect

  • Name searches (Pro only): First name, last name, optional city and state. Used only to query unclaimed-property databases on your behalf. We do not store these searches server-side beyond transient request logs.
  • Email address: Collected when you subscribe to Pro, restore access, or opt in to deadline alerts. Used to deliver notifications and Pro account access.
  • Push subscription data: If you enable browser push notifications, we store the subscription endpoint and encryption keys needed to deliver alerts.
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number, CVV, or full billing details.
  • Claim activity: Which settlements you marked as claimed, stored locally and server-side to keep your list in sync.
  • Device and log data: IP address, browser type, and request timestamps for security and rate limiting. IPs are used for abuse prevention only and are not sold or shared.

How We Use Your Information

  • To perform unclaimed-property searches you explicitly request
  • To process and manage your Pro subscription through Stripe
  • To send deadline alerts you have opted in to receive
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and security incidents
  • To improve the service (aggregated, non-identifying analytics only)

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone
  • We do not share your name or email with third parties except as required to deliver the service (Stripe, push notification services, unclaimed-property databases you search)
  • We do not use your data to train AI models
  • We do not track you across other sites

Third Parties We Use

  • Stripe — payment processing (their policy)
  • MissingMoney.com (NAUPA) — multi-state unclaimed property search, when you initiate a name search
  • State unclaimed property databases — when you click through to check a specific state

Data Retention

We keep Pro subscription and email preference data as long as your account is active. If you cancel, we retain billing records required by law and delete the rest within 30 days of your request. Push subscriptions are removed immediately when you unsubscribe or uninstall.

Your Rights

You can request deletion, correction, or export of your personal data at any time. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we have collected and to request its deletion. Contact us using the information below.

Security

We use HTTPS for all traffic, rate-limit every endpoint, validate and sanitize all input, and store only the minimum data necessary to provide the service. Passwords and session tokens use industry-standard hashing. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your data like it's our own.

Children

This service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify Pro subscribers by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
